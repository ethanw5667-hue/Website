What I do

I’m passionate about modeling real-world physics and then building rigs to test the model.

I work from first principles—optimizing wing shapes, modeling hysteresis in air muscles, and designing reproducible setups with clear methods. Projects span a prototype app that identifies stars and estimates sky glow, small biomedical engineering builds, and IOAA prep guides in celestial mechanics.
